About Me

Hello! I'm an Engine/Gameplay Programmer with a strong game development background from Digital Arts and Entertainment. My portfolio includes work in AI, procedural generation, and graphics rendering.

I'm efficient in C++ and C# and have working in multiple engines such as Unity, Unreal Engine and even wrote a few custom ones which used DirectX, OpenGL and Vulkan. I also have experience in GPU programming which includes shaders, Compute Shaders and CUDA programming.

I am known for my fast and efficient work style, and I thrive on solving problems in creative and technical ways. I am passionate about finding solutions that enhance my skills and improve the overall quality of my projects.
